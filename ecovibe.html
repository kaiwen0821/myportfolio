EcoVibe is a high-fidelity sustainable e-commerce website designed to promote eco-friendly shopping through a clean, modern, and intuitive user experience.

Problem

Many e-commerce platforms lack clear sustainability messaging and often feel cluttered, making it hard for users to shop consciously and efficiently.

Solution

EcoVibe provides a streamlined shopping experience with clear product categorisation, wishlist functionality, and an intuitive checkout flow, encouraging users to make environmentally responsible purchasing decisions.

Design Focus

Designed as a UI/UX project in Figma, EcoVibe focuses on clean layouts, eco-inspired visuals, responsive design, and smooth interactions such as dynamic banners and engaging product displays.

Key Screens

Home Page, Product Categories, Wishlist, Product Details, Checkout

Tech Stack:

  • Design & Prototyping: Figma
  • UI Components & Assets: Unsplash images, Icons8
