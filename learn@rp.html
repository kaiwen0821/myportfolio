Learn@RP is a high-fidelity mobile learning app designed to help Republic Polytechnic students upskill conveniently and independently through short, engaging learning content.

Problem

Many existing learning platforms are not tailored to RP students and are difficult to use on the go, making independent upskilling inconvenient and unmotivating.

Solution

Learn@RP provides personalised mini-courses with short video lessons, allowing students to explore industry-relevant skills beyond their academic modules while tracking their progress flexibly.

Design Focus

Designed as part of the C218 UI/UX Design for Apps module, the app features a fully customised UI kit, consistent visual styling, intuitive navigation, and meaningful micro-interactions.

Key Screens

Home Screen, Course List, Lesson Details

Tech Stack:

  • Design & Prototyping: Figma
  • UI Components & Assets: Unsplash images, Icons8
